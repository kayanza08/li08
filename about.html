About Us

University of Dodoma (UDOM) is one of the leading higher education institutions in Tanzania, established with the mission of providing comprehensive, inclusive, and accessible higher education, research, and public service. Located in the city of Dodoma, UDOM aims to foster knowledge, innovation, and a spirit of inquiry among students and faculty alike.

Mission

The mission of UDOM is to create a conducive environment for teaching, learning, research, and public service through the provision of comprehensive, accessible, and affordable higher education that meets the needs of the community and the nation.

Vision

UDOM's vision is to become a center of excellence in higher education and research, recognized nationally and internationally for its commitment to quality education and contribution to societal development.

History

Established in 2007, the University of Dodoma has quickly grown to become one of the largest universities in Tanzania. With a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, UDOM attracts students from all over the country and beyond. The university is committed to fostering academic excellence and providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers and contribute to the development of their communities.
