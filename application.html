Application

Welcome to the University of Dodoma's application page. Here you will find all the necessary information and steps to apply for our programs.

Undergraduate Applications

Prospective undergraduate students are required to complete the following steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form on our official website.
  2. Submit certified copies of your academic transcripts and certificates.
  3. Provide proof of payment for the application fee.
  4. Submit a personal statement or essay as required by the specific program.

Postgraduate Applications

Prospective postgraduate students must follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form available on our official website.
  2. Submit certified copies of your undergraduate transcripts and degree certificates.
  3. Provide two academic references.
  4. Submit a detailed research proposal or statement of purpose.
  5. Provide proof of payment for the application fee.

International Students

International applicants must also complete the following additional steps:

  1. Provide proof of English language proficiency (e.g., TOEFL, IELTS).
  2. Submit a copy of your passport.
  3. Complete any additional requirements specific to your country of origin.

Application Deadlines

Please note the following application deadlines:

  • Undergraduate Programs: March 31st for the Fall semester, September 30th for the Spring semester.
  • Postgraduate Programs: April 30th for the Fall semester, October 31st for the Spring semester.
